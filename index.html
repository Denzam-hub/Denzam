INTRODUCTION

The United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SGs) are 17 interconnected objectives designed to transform our world by 2030.

Adopted by 193countries, the SDGs address pressing global challenges, such as poverty, inequality, climate change, and social injustice.

These amitious goals seek to ensure a sustainable, equitable, and peaceful future for all

FOCUS: CLIMATE ACTION (SDG 13)

SDG 13: Climate Action aiims to combat climate change and its devasting impacts. Rising global temperatures threaten ecosystems, human health, and economic stability. Urgent action is necessary to:

1. strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards.

2. integrate climate change measures into national policies.

3. promote education, awarteness, and international cooperation.
