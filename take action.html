Climate Change Impacts:

1. Sea-level rise and coastal erosion

2. Extreme weather events (heatwaves, droughts,floods)

3. Water scarcity and food insecurity

4. Loss of biodiversity and ecosystem disruption

solutions

1. Transition to renewable energy sources(solar, wind and hydro)

2. Energy efficiency and conservation

3. Electrify transportation and industry

4. Carbon capture and storage

5. Sustainable land use and Forestry
